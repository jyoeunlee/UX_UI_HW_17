hi, I'm Joeun Lee

A graphic designer based in Los Angeles, specializing in User Interface Design. Joeun enjoys creating memories and keeping a record using photography and videography. She is happiest when she takes photos, works on videos, and designs things. Without any of her creating habits, her world would be very boring.

her story:

Joeun designed just for fun. In her high school years, she took art, ceramics, and video production just to enjoy various forms of art. She never thought art related job could be something she can do for her entire life. In college, she was a mathematics major. As usual, she took an art class for her electives just to earn an easy A credit. The art professor she met during the elective course told her, “Don't let your talent go to waste.” And that words inspired her to take art more serious and motivated to change the direction of her life. Consequently, Joeun earned A.A degree and B.A degree in Art. Her interests in various medium of arts (graphic design, painting, ceramics, sculpture, photography, video production, figure drawing, etc) lead her to decide which form of art she will do for her occupation or for her entertainment. After graduation, Joeun started as a graphic designer at a lifestyle store. She designs lots of different things including advertisement, flyer, banner, signage, email graphic, apparel graphic, giftcard and so much more. She is now a creative director who is in charge of designs and she fulfills any visual needs for store website. And in order to understand more about website design, she achieves a certificate from UX/UI design bootcamp.
